# Smart Coffee Machine – Full User Guide

## Product Overview
The ShopAssist Smart Coffee Machine is an automatic espresso and drip coffee maker with programmable brewing settings.

---

## 1. Setup Instructions

### First Use
1. Remove packaging materials
2. Wash water tank and drip tray
3. Fill water tank (max line)
4. Plug into power outlet

---

## 2. Control Panel

- Power Button
- Espresso Mode
- Cappuccino Mode
- Custom Brew
- Temperature Control
- Timer Settings

---

## 3. Brewing Coffee

### Espresso
1. Add coffee grounds (fine grind)
2. Press Espresso button
3. Wait 30–45 seconds

### Cappuccino
1. Fill milk container
2. Select Cappuccino mode
3. Machine froths milk automatically

---

## 4. Cleaning & Maintenance

### Daily
- Empty drip tray
- Rinse milk container

### Weekly
- Run descaling cycle
- Clean filter basket

### Monthly
- Deep clean water tank

---

## 5. Descaling Process
1. Fill tank with descaling solution
2. Run “Clean Mode”
3. Rinse 2 cycles with clean water

---

## 6. Safety Warnings
- Do not open during brewing
- Avoid touching hot steam nozzle
- Always unplug before cleaning
