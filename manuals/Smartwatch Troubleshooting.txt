# Smartwatch Troubleshooting

## Not pairing
- Restart Bluetooth
- Restart both devices
- Ensure app permissions enabled

## Battery draining fast
- Disable GPS when not needed
- Reduce brightness
- Turn off always-on display

## Heart rate inaccurate
- Clean sensor
- Wear snugly on wrist
- Avoid movement during measurement

## Notifications not showing
- Check app permissions
- Enable background sync
