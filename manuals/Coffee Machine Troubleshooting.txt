# Coffee Machine Troubleshooting

## Problem: Machine not turning on
- Check power outlet
- Inspect fuse

## Problem: Coffee too weak
- Increase coffee dosage
- Use finer grind

## Problem: No water flow
- Water tank empty
- Blocked filter

## Problem: Loud noise
- Air trapped in pump
- Run water cycle

## Error Codes
- E01 → Water tank not detected
- E02 → Overheating
- E03 → Blocked filter
