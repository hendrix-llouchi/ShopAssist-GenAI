# Air Fryer – Complete User Guide

## Overview
High-efficiency air fryer using rapid hot-air circulation.

---

## 1. Setup
- Place on flat surface
- Ensure ventilation space (10cm around)
- Plug into power outlet

---

## 2. Cooking Instructions

### Basic Cooking
1. Preheat for 3–5 minutes
2. Place food in basket
3. Set temperature (160°C–200°C)
4. Set timer (1–30 minutes)
5. Shake basket halfway for even cooking

---

## 3. Cooking Tips
- Do not overcrowd basket
- Light oil improves crispiness
- Use parchment paper for easy cleanup

---

## 4. Cleaning
- Let cool for 20 minutes
- Wash basket with warm water
- Wipe interior with soft cloth

---

## 5. Safety
- Do not submerge in water
- Do not block air vents
- Avoid metal utensils inside basket
