# Wireless Headphones – Troubleshooting Guide

## Issue 1: Headphones not turning on

### Possible causes:
- Battery completely drained
- Faulty charging cable

### Solutions:
- Charge for at least 30 minutes
- Try a different USB-C cable

---

## Issue 2: Bluetooth not connecting

### Fix steps:
1. Turn Bluetooth OFF and ON
2. Reset headphones (hold power 10 seconds)
3. Forget device and re-pair

---

## Issue 3: One side not working

### Causes:
- Audio balance issue
- Hardware imbalance

### Fix:
- Check device audio balance settings
- Reset headphones
- Test on another device

---

## Issue 4: Audio lag during video

### Fix:
- Move closer to device
- Avoid interference (WiFi routers, microwaves)
- Update Bluetooth drivers (PC)

---

## Issue 5: Low sound quality

### Fix:
- Disable ANC and test
- Ensure clean ear cushions
- Reconnect Bluetooth

---

## Advanced Diagnostic Mode
Hold Volume + and Power for 8 seconds to enter diagnostic mode.
