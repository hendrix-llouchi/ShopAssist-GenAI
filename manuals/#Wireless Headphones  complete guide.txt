# Wireless Headphones – Complete User Guide

## Product Overview
ShopAssist Wireless Headphones are over-ear Bluetooth headphones designed for music, calls, and gaming with active noise cancellation (ANC).

---

## 1. Initial Setup

### What’s in the box
- Wireless Headphones
- USB-C Charging Cable
- 3.5mm Audio Cable
- User Manual

---

## 2. Power Functions

### Turn On
- Hold the power button for **3 seconds**
- You will hear: "Power On"
- LED turns blue

### Turn Off
- Hold power button for **5 seconds**
- You will hear: "Power Off"

### Reset Device
- Hold power button for **10–12 seconds**
- LED flashes red and blue alternately

---

## 3. Bluetooth Pairing

### First Time Pairing
1. Turn on headphones
2. Hold power button for 5–7 seconds until LED flashes red/blue
3. On your phone:
   - Go to Bluetooth settings
   - Select: **ShopAssist Headphones X1**
4. Wait for “Connected” voice prompt

### Reconnecting
- Device automatically reconnects to last paired device

---

## 4. Audio Controls

- Volume Up: Tap “+” button
- Volume Down: Tap “-” button
- Play/Pause: Single press power button
- Next Track: Double press power button
- Previous Track: Triple press power button

---

## 5. Charging

- Use USB-C port only
- Charging time: ~2 hours
- Battery life:
  - Music: 18–22 hours
  - Calls: 15 hours
  - Standby: 200 hours

### LED Indicators
- Red blinking → Low battery
- Solid red → Charging
- Green → Fully charged

---

## 6. Active Noise Cancellation (ANC)

- Press ANC button once to toggle:
  - ANC ON → blocks background noise
  - ANC OFF → normal mode
  - Transparency Mode → allows external sound

---

## 7. Safety Instructions

- Do not use at high volume for extended periods
- Do not expose to water or heavy moisture
- Do not bend headband beyond natural range

---
