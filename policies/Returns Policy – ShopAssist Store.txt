# Returns Policy – ShopAssist Store

## Overview
We aim to provide a hassle-free return experience for all customers.

---

## 1. Return Eligibility

You are eligible to return an item if:

- The item was purchased within the last **14 days**
- The item is in **original condition**
- All accessories and packaging are included

---

## 2. Non-Returnable Items

- Personal hygiene products
- Gift cards
- Items marked “Final Sale”
- Products damaged due to misuse

---

## 3. Condition Requirements

Returns will be rejected if:

- Severe physical damage is detected
- Missing parts or accessories
- Signs of tampering

---

## 4. Return Verification

All returns undergo:
- Photo verification (AI-based)
- Manual inspection if flagged

---

## 5. Refund Timeline

- Approved returns: 3–7 business days
- Bank processing: additional 1–3 days

---

## 6. Shipping Responsibility

- Customer pays return shipping unless item is defective
