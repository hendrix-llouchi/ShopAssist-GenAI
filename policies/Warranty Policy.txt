# Warranty Policy

## Coverage Period
- Electronics: 12 months
- Accessories: 6 months

---

## Covered

- Manufacturing defects
- Hardware malfunctions

---

## Not Covered

- Accidental damage
- Water damage
- Unauthorized repairs
- Normal wear and tear

---

## Warranty Claim Process

1. Submit order ID
2. Upload product image
3. AI pre-check
4. Manual review if required
